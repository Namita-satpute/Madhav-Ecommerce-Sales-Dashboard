🛒 Madhav Ecommerce Sales Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

The Madhav Ecommerce Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze ecommerce sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, profit trends, and payment method preferences. The dashboard provides business insights through dynamic KPIs, category analysis, state-wise sales performance, and profit tracking.

🎯 Project Objective

This dashboard helps answer key business questions:

Which states generate the highest sales?
Which product categories contribute the most revenue?
What are the most profitable sub-categories?
Which payment methods are preferred by customers?
How does profit vary over time?
Who are the top customers?

💡 Business Insights
Maharashtra contributes the highest sales revenue.
Clothing category generates the largest sales volume.
COD remains the most preferred payment method.
Printers are the most profitable product sub-category.
Sales and profits show significant variation across days, indicating seasonal demand patterns.
